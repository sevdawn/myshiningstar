Who am I?

Oh dear. Grab a cup of coffee (or something stronger, I'm partial to bourbon myself) and sit a spell. Wife, Mother, Sagittarian, Dragon, I am all of that and more. By night, I work for a local Amazon FC. Inbound is my home department, but I'm crosstrained all over the building so I never know where I'll end up throughout the night. During the day, when I'm not catching up on my beauty sleep, taking care of my three kids (my husband totally counts, he said so himself, so that makes 3) and the two cats, I'm learning how to be a front end web developer. I also admin a FB fan page for the Swedish metal band Avatar. I am quite well known as dressing up as AVATAR's vocalist Johannes Eckerström for shows and have given myself the title of Lady Gin Swift, Bard of the Freakshow. I also stream on Twitch from time to time, you can watch me be a filthy casual gamer.

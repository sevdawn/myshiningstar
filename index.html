Welcome to Sevdawn Photography!

As I say all of the time, 'I'm no pro photog, just a fan with a smartphone' The majority of my photos are of one band in particular, the Swedish metal band AVATAR. This site (very much a work in progress, so bear with me) will be a showcase of my photos of this incredible band. The background pic of this site is one I snapped Sept 2019 in Lexington, KY. Click on the 'Flickr Gallery' button to see a small sample of my work photographing this incredible band.

All the way from Gothenburg, Sweden

I first discovered the Swedish metal band AVATAR in Oct 2014 at the first Louder than Life festival in Louisville KY. As of Sept 2019, I've seen them 25 times across 7 states in everywhere from hole in the wall bars to massive music festivals. I've taken hundreds of pics, made countless friends, and created memories that will last a lifetime.

About Me
Oh dear. Grab a cup of coffee (or something stronger, I'm partial to bourbon myself) and sit a spell. Wife, Mother, Sagittarian, Dragon, I am all of that and more. By night, I work for a local Amazon FC. Inbound is my home department, but I'm crosstrained all over the building so I never know where I'll end up throughout the night. During the day, when I'm not catching up on my beauty sleep, taking care of my three kids (my husband totally counts, he said so himself, so that makes 3) and the two cats, I'm learning how to be a front end web developer.

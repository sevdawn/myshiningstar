Welcome to Sevdawn Photography!

I am NOT a pro photographer. I say all the time that 'I'm no pro photog, just a fan with a smartphone' That said, over the years I've accumulated many incredible pics with just a smartphone, GIMP photo editor, and LOTS of trial and error. 'Crazy Stories' takes you to a page detailing just six of the countless stories I've accumulated over the years. 'Gallery' button takes you to two galleries I have uploaded to Flickr. I am just starting on my journey of chronicling my photos as I have quite literally hundreds of photos I've snapped in venues all over the US since 2015.

All the way from Gothenburg, Sweden

I first discovered the Swedish metal band AVATAR in Oct 2014 at the first Louder than Life festival in Louisville KY. As of Sept 2019, I've seen them 25 times across 7 states in everywhere from hole in the wall bars to massive music festivals. I've taken hundreds of pics taken, made countless friends, and memories that will last a lifetime.

So I've seen AVATAR a few times (this is just since 2018)

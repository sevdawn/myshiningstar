Welcome to Sevdawn Photography!

As I say all of the time, 'I'm no pro photog, just a fan with a smartphone' The majority of my photos are of one band in particular, the Swedish metal band AVATAR. This site (very much a work in progress, so bear with me) will be a showcase of my photos of this incredible band. The background pic of this site is one I snapped Sept 2019 in Lexington, KY. Click on the 'Flickr Gallery' button to see a small sample of my work photographing this incredible band.

All the way from Gothenburg, Sweden

I first discovered the Swedish metal band AVATAR in Oct 2014 at the first Louder than Life festival in Louisville KY. As of Sept 2019, I've seen them 25 times across 7 states in everywhere from hole in the wall bars to massive music festivals. I've taken hundreds of pics taken, made countless friends, and memories that will last a lifetime.
